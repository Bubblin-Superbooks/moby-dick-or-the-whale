"Landlord," said I, going up to him as cool as Mt. Hecla in a snowstorm—"landlord, stop whittling. You and I must understand one another, and that too without delay. I come to your house and want a bed; you tell me you can only give me half a one; that the other half belongs to a certain harpooneer.
